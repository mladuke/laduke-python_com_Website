Rick Allen

The Thunder God

Rick Allen live with Def Leppard on 13 August 2008 in Winnipeg.

Here's a timeline of Rick Allen's life:

  • 1963 - Born in Derbyshire , UK
  • 1972 - Started playing drums at age 9
  • 1978 - Joins the hard rock band "Def Leppard"
  • 1980 - Def Leppard releases first album , On Through the Night.
  • 1984 - Thrown from car, servering left arm, and breaking right shoulder
  • 1986 - Returns to performing with Def Leppard, using an electronic drum kit
  • 1987 - Def Leppard releases Hysteria, a highly acclaimed album
  • 1991 - Marries Stacy Lauren Gilbert
  • 1995 - Arrested for spousal battery
  • 1997 - Has a daughter, Lauren Shane Allen
  • 2000 - Divorces Stacy
  • 2001 - Forms the Raven Drum Foundation, whose purpose is to educate and empower individuals and communities in crisis through healing arts programs, drumming events and collaborative partnerships
  • 2003 - Marries Lauren Monroe
  • 2010 - Has a second daughter, Josefine Allen
"Surviving a car accident and losing my arm could have destroyed my life. Instead it led me on an unexpected path of finding my deepest strength." --Rick Allen
